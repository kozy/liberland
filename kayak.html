Under the Liberland flag, the Wildwater Canoe Slalom Club Team boasts today of having have achieve some of these below:

3rd place in the championship of the Liberec region in Česká Lípa, Czech Republic.
3rd place of the Regional Championship of Trutnov, Krkonoše, Czech Republic.

Of course, the main ambition of the team is to go much further - as Ing. David Horák, founding father of the team’s wish is, to expand across borders. His goal is to recruit more Champions to the team and plans to get financial support so that they are ready to conquer Europe and in the two following years take on Tokyo Olympics!
