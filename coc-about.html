About the Chamber

Liberland Chamber of Commerce is an independent entity, associating members based on voluntary principles and mutual business interests. It represents the main building block in the future global network of bilateral chambers, connecting businesses between Liberland and all respective countries.

Chamber of Commerce is following its key goals (see full Statute)
  • Promotion of Liberland entrepreneurs
  • Facilitation of undertaking of business activity
  • Facilitation of commercial contacts between Liberland and other entrepreneurs;
  • Representation of common interests of the members of the Chamber
  • Promotion of economic, social and cultural cooperation between Liberland and respective countries.
  • Promotion, support and seeking opportunities to economic development of the whole Slavonia region

Member’s rights

  • use services of the Chamber and participate in any of its operations
  • submit motions pertaining to the Chamber operations,
  • participate in the General Meetings,
  • active and passive electoral rights pertaining to the Board of the Chamber.

Member’s obligations

  • base their operations on principles of professional ethics and standards of fair conduct,
  • observe the provisions of the Statutes of the Chamber as well as the regulations and resolutions of the General Meetings and Board of the Chamber,
  • participate in the performance of the Chamber statutory objectives,
  • consider participating by a regular voluntary membership fee

Members may, but not have to be registered in Liberland jurisdiction.

Regular membership is subject to an annual voluntary fee

Main activities of the Chamber and benefits

  • establishing and developing economic cooperation with domestic and foreign entities
  • opening bilateral Chambers of Commerce between Liberland and respective countries
  • collecting, analysing and distributing information supporting business operations of its members;
  • offering support for its members' business initiatives,
  • organizing and co-organizing fairs, exhibitions, conferences, meetings or webinars in Liberland and abroad
